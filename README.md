[CS50x](https://pll.harvard.edu/course/cs50-introduction-computer-science), is Harvard University's introduction to the intellectual enterprises of **computer science** and the art of programming. An _entry-level_ course taught by **David J. Malan** (@dmalan), CS50x teaches students _how to think algorithmically_ and _solve problems efficiently_.
- Inside this repository, you will find *my* (@Xartei) solutions to the **PSETS** and **Final Project**.
> _The solutions inside of this repo are based in the 2026 edition of the course. If you somehow found this repo and found any of the solutions helpful, leave a ⭐! The solutions were written **100%** without AI_. C;
